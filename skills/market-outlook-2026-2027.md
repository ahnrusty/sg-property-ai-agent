# Singapore Property Market Outlook 2026 – 2027

Snapshot as of May 2026. Refresh every quarter when URA / HDB flash estimates are released. Always re-verify before quoting.

## Headline forecast (base case)

| Segment | 2026 full-year | 2027 outlook | Drivers |
|---------|----------------|---------------|---------|
| URA Private PPI (overall) | +2.5% to +4.5% | flat to +3% | Easing rates, record completions, ABSD intact |
| OCR non-landed | +4% to +6% | +2% to +4% | HDB upgrader demand, MRT-linked launches |
| RCR non-landed | +3% to +5% | +2% to +3% | Owner-occupier core, rental support |
| CCR non-landed | flat to +2% | flat to +2% | 60% foreigner ABSD suppresses luxury |
| HDB Resale Index | flat to +4% (Q1 2026 flash −0.1% QoQ) | flat to +2% | MOP wave + 4,000 shorter-wait BTO/year |
| Private rental index | flat to −2% | flat | Record completions, expat normalisation |

Source basis: URA Q1 2026 release, OrangeTee / Realion 2026 outlook, ERA, Huttons, BT, ST.

## Hard numbers anchoring the outlook

- **URA PPI Q1 2026**: +0.9% QoQ final (vs flash +0.3%). 6th straight quarter of growth. Non-landed +1.3%, landed −1.8%. OCR led at +2.2%.
- **Transactions Q1 2026**: ~4,041 deals, −39.7% QoQ. Likely cyclical low; rebound expected as 2Q–4Q pipeline of ~8,892 units lands.
- **HDB Resale Index Q1 2026**: −0.1% QoQ. First quarterly decline in nearly 7 years.
- **Total private pipeline**: ~55,800 units (incl ECs); ~12,400 completions in 2026 (~65% above 10-year average); ~10,800 completions in 2027.
- **New launches 2026**: ~17 projects / ~8,100 units (down ~30% YoY from 2025).
- **2027 reload**: from sites awarded 2025 (Bedok Rise ~380, Telok Blangah Rd ~740, Dorset Rd ~430, Upper Thomson Parcel A ~595, Lentor Gardens ~530, Jurong Lake District ~800 to 1,000).
- **1H 2026 GLS**: 4,575 Confirmed (incl 635 EC) + 4,610 Reserve = ~9,185 potential units. 50% above 10-year half-yearly average.

## HDB BTO supply 2025 – 2027 (locked in)

- 55,000 BTO flats across 2025 to 2027 (10% above earlier 50k target)
- 2026 launches: 19,600 flats across Feb / Jun / Oct; ~4,000 with shorter wait (< 3 yrs)
- 2027 launches: ~15,000+ flats; another ~4,000 shorter-wait
- MOP wave (resale supply unlock): 8k (2025) → **13,484 (2026)** → **18,939 (2027)** → 21,393 (2028)

## Mortgage / rate outlook

- 3M compounded SORA: ~1.0 to 1.2% Q1 2026
- Forecast floor ~1.0% by Q2 2026, drift to ~1.30 to 1.40% by end-2026 (UOB / MAS-aligned consensus)
- 2027: expected to stay below 2% absent a Fed shock
- Indicative bank packages (May 2026): floating SORA + 0.6 to 0.8% ≈ 1.7 to 2.0%; 2 to 3-yr fixed ~1.45 to 2.0%
- Refinancing window: loans maturing 2026 to 2027 can pick up ~150 to 200 bps savings vs 2023 peak. Compare every 12 months.

## Cooling measures in force (no 2026 changes announced)

- **ABSD** (from 27 Apr 2023): SC 0 / 20 / 30%, SPR 5 / 30 / 35%, Foreigner 60% on any, Entity / Trust 65%.
- **TDSR 55%, MSR 30%** (HDB and EC).
- **LTV**: HDB loan 75%, bank loan 75% on first; 45% / 25% on 2nd / 3rd.
- **15-month wait-out** for private owners buying non-subsidised HDB resale (likely removed *only* when resale prices stabilise, per MND).
- **HDB Plus / Prime framework** (from Oct 2024 BTO): Standard (5-yr MOP, no clawback), Plus (10-yr MOP, 6 to 8% clawback), Prime (10-yr MOP, ~9% clawback, SC-only resale, no whole-unit rental).

## Risks to base case

**Upside**:
- Faster Fed cuts pushing SORA below 1%
- GLS underbidding (developers cautious)
- Middle East / trade shock driving safe-haven capital into SG
- ABSD relaxation (not signalled)

**Downside**:
- Prolonged US/China slowdown hitting SG hiring
- Construction cost spike
- Supply landing faster than absorbed
- Currency shock pushing SORA back above 2.5%

## Segment plays for 2026 – 2027

- **HDB upgraders**: 5-room sellers from 2014–2018 cohort still walk away with ~$500–600k after loan, enough for ~$2M condo down payment. HDB softening is uncomfortable for HDB sellers, not fatal for the upgrader pipeline.
- **OCR new launch**: strongest momentum (Tampines, Bedok, Upper Thomson, Lentor). Watch take-up > 70% on launch weekend as a quality signal.
- **RCR**: Greater Southern Waterfront, Queenstown, Bayshore — owner-occupier sweet spot.
- **CCR**: best entry for citizens / PRs willing to hold; foreigner ABSD keeps prices flat, useful as a first own-stay.
- **EC**: high MSR-eligible demand; income ceiling still $16k. Limited 2026 EC pipeline (Canberra Drive, Sembawang Drive, Miltonia Close ~430).
- **Rental**: tenant-favourable in 2026, especially older non-integrated condos. Lock 24-month with diplomatic clause where possible.
