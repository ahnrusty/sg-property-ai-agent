# HDB Eligibility

Covers BTO, SBF, resale, schemes, grants, and key conditions. Re-verify on hdb.gov.sg before applying.

## Schemes (you must qualify under one)

| Scheme | Who |
|--------|-----|
| Public Scheme | SC + at least 1 SC / SPR family member; spouse, parents, children, siblings |
| Fiance / Fiancee | Engaged couple (one SC); must produce marriage cert within 3 months of key collection |
| Single Singapore Citizen | Single SC ≥ 35 yrs; can buy 2-room Flexi (BTO) or any resale |
| Joint Singles | 2 to 4 single SCs ≥ 35 yrs jointly |
| Non-Citizen Spouse | SC + non-SC spouse; resale only (BTO not allowed) |
| Non-Citizen Family | Non-SC parent/sibling; resale only |
| Orphans | SC orphans ≥ 21 yrs |
| Conversion | Adjoining flats in same block for multi-gen |

## Income ceilings (current; verify)

| Application | Family ceiling | Singles ceiling |
|-------------|----------------|------------------|
| BTO 2-room Flexi (short lease) | $14,000 | $14,000 |
| BTO 3-room and above | $14,000 (or $21,000 for extended families) | n/a |
| Executive Condominium (EC) | $16,000 | n/a |
| HDB resale (with HDB loan eligibility) | $14,000 | $7,000 |

## Minimum Occupation Period (MOP)

| Flat type | MOP |
|-----------|-----|
| BTO Standard (from Oct 2024 framework) | 5 years |
| BTO Plus | 10 years |
| BTO Prime | 10 years |
| Resale flat purchased without grant | 5 years |
| Resale flat purchased with CPF Housing Grant | 5 years |
| DBSS, ROF Public Housing | 5 years |
| EC (5-year MOP from key collection) | 5 years |

MOP counts physical occupation. Letting out the whole flat during MOP is not allowed without HDB special approval.

## BTO classification framework (from Oct 2024)

| Tier | MOP | Subsidy clawback | Resale rules |
|------|-----|------------------|--------------|
| Standard | 5 yrs | None | Standard EIP / SPR quota |
| Plus | 10 yrs | 6–8% of resale price | SC and SPR only; income ceiling at resale ($14k) |
| Prime | 10 yrs | ~9% of resale price | SC only; income ceiling at resale; no whole-flat rental |

Subsidy clawback is computed on the higher of resale price or valuation at sale.

## Ethnic Integration Policy (EIP) and SPR quota

Each block and neighbourhood has caps on the proportion of Chinese, Malay, Indian & Others, and SPR households. The HDB Resale Portal flags whether your purchase will breach the cap. If breached, you cannot buy; check before paying any option fee.

## Resale process (high level)

1. Both buyer and seller register Intent to Buy / Sell on HDB Resale Portal.
2. Buyer obtains HFE Letter (HDB Flat Eligibility) or bank IPA.
3. Buyer issues OTP; pays option fee ($1 to $1,000, agreed between parties).
4. Buyer exercises OTP within 21 days; pays balance of deposit (typical total deposit $5,000).
5. Buyer submits resale application via HDB Resale Portal (both buyer and seller submit their portions).
6. HDB processes (~8 weeks); both parties attend completion appointment.
7. Keys collected on completion day.

## Cash Over Valuation (COV)

After OTP, request HDB valuation. If agreed price > valuation, the difference is COV and must be paid in cash. CPF and bank loan only cover up to valuation. Practically, negotiate price down to valuation if possible.

## Grants (for SC buyers; subject to scheme and conditions)

- **Enhanced CPF Housing Grant (EHG)**: up to $120,000 for families, $60,000 for singles, based on monthly household income (≤ $9,000 family / ≤ $4,500 single for full grant).
- **CPF Housing Grant (Resale)**: up to $80,000 for families, $40,000 for singles (income ≤ $14k / $7k).
- **Proximity Housing Grant (PHG)**: $30,000 if buying near parents (within 4km); $20,000 if buying to live with parents.
- **Step-Up CPF Housing Grant**: for second-time applicants buying 3-room or larger.

Grants are paid into CPF OA and form part of the down payment / mortgage offset. Subject to scheme, income, and other conditions.

## 15-month wait-out

Private residential property owners (current or within last 15 months) who buy a non-subsidised HDB resale flat must wait 15 months after disposing of their private property. This rule was introduced in Sep 2022 and is still in force.

Exception: seniors aged 55 and above buying a 4-room or smaller HDB resale flat are exempted.

## Renting out

- Whole-flat rental: only after MOP. Whole-flat rental requires HDB approval; lease term 6 months to 3 years for SC tenants, 6 to 24 months for non-citizens.
- Bedroom rental: allowed during MOP for 3-room and above. Owner must continue to live in the flat. Maximum 6 occupants per flat (including owner).
