# Due Diligence Checklist

Use the relevant section. Mark each item Verified / Pending / Not Applicable.

## Universal

- [ ] Title and ownership match seller name on IC or company UEN
- [ ] No caveats, mortgages, or encumbrances disclosed
- [ ] Seller motivation understood (relocating, upgrading, financial)
- [ ] Listing is exclusive or open (affects negotiation leverage)
- [ ] Recent transacted prices for nearby units pulled (URA Realis or HDB Resale Statistics)
- [ ] URA Master Plan checked for future zoning, MRT, roadworks, en-bloc activity
- [ ] News search for the building, street, and surrounding area
- [ ] Noise check: nearby construction, expressway, MRT line, schools, places of worship
- [ ] Floods or other infrastructure issues in the area

## HDB resale

- [ ] MOP satisfied (5 years for Standard; 10 years for Plus / Prime; check date of key collection)
- [ ] Ethnic Integration Policy (EIP) and Singapore Permanent Resident (SPR) quota allow your household
- [ ] Income ceiling check if applying for HDB loan (currently $14k household for families, $7k singles)
- [ ] HDB loan eligibility (HLE) letter obtained, or bank IPA
- [ ] Valuation request submitted via HDB Resale Portal (after OTP)
- [ ] Cash Over Valuation (COV) understanding — must be paid in cash
- [ ] Upgrading levy applicability (if seller benefited from upgrading programmes)
- [ ] Outstanding HDB conservancy charges and property tax cleared by seller
- [ ] Inspection of flat condition: structural, electrical, plumbing, ceiling, windows, parquet
- [ ] Renovation Permit check if seller did major works

## Private condo (resale)

- [ ] MCST (Management Corporation Strata Title) minutes for last 2 years
- [ ] Sinking fund balance and 5-year capital expenditure plan
- [ ] Special levy history and any pending levies
- [ ] Pending or recent litigation involving MCST
- [ ] Owner-occupier ratio (high tenant ratio can affect community feel and rules enforcement)
- [ ] By-laws on pets, short-term rental, renovation hours, parking
- [ ] En-bloc history: any failed attempts, current EGM activity
- [ ] Common area condition: pool, gym, lift, security, landscaping
- [ ] Unit-specific: leaks, ceiling stains, balcony seepage, window seals, aircon ledge
- [ ] Stack-specific: facing, noise (pool deck, road, BBQ pit, MRT line)
- [ ] Building defects history if under DLP

## Private condo (new launch)

- [ ] Developer track record (past projects, on-time delivery, build quality)
- [ ] Sales gallery showflat vs as-built drawings: ceiling height, finishes, balcony depth
- [ ] Strata vs internal vs aircon ledge area breakdown
- [ ] Progressive Payment Scheme schedule and your cashflow plan
- [ ] Defects Liability Period coverage (typically 12 months)
- [ ] Date of Notice of Vacant Possession (estimated TOP) — note that delays are common
- [ ] ABSD timing: if buying second property, plan first-property sale within remission window
- [ ] Carpark allocation: 1 per unit usually, EV charging readiness
- [ ] Maintenance fee estimate (often inflates 10–20% in first AGM)
- [ ] Future neighbouring developments that may block your facing

## Executive Condominium

- [ ] Income ceiling at application ($16k household)
- [ ] At least one Singapore Citizen in household
- [ ] No ownership of another local or overseas property in the 30 months before
- [ ] 5-year MOP for resale to SC / PR
- [ ] 10-year point for full privatisation (resale to foreigners)
- [ ] Subsidy clawback / resale levy applicability

## Landed

- [ ] Title type (freehold, 999, 99) and any remaining lease
- [ ] Foreigner ownership eligibility (SLA approval required outside Sentosa Cove)
- [ ] Plot size, plot ratio, GFA potential for rebuild / extension
- [ ] Conservation status (some areas under URA conservation rules)
- [ ] Setback requirements, building line, party wall agreements
- [ ] Soil conditions, history of subsidence or flooding
- [ ] Easements, drainage reserves, MRT tunnel reserves below ground

## Rental (tenant)

- [ ] Landlord identity verified (IC matches title search via SLA)
- [ ] Tenancy Agreement reviewed (diplomatic clause, deposit, minor repair cap, inventory)
- [ ] Inventory and condition list signed by both parties with photos
- [ ] Stamp duty paid (0.4% of total rent for ≤ 4-year lease)
- [ ] HDB whole-flat sublet has HDB approval (if HDB)
- [ ] Condo MCST allows rental (by-laws and minimum tenure)
- [ ] Utilities account transfer plan and meter reading at handover
- [ ] Aircon servicing arrangement and frequency

## Financial

- [ ] Bank IPA secured, fixed or floating package chosen
- [ ] BSD computed
- [ ] ABSD computed and timing of payment understood
- [ ] CPF OA usage projected (and accrued interest implications)
- [ ] Cash buffer ≥ 6 months of mortgage + maintenance after closing
- [ ] Property tax estimate (annual value × tax rate per progressive band)
- [ ] Fire insurance (mandatory for mortgaged property)
- [ ] Mortgage insurance (HPS for HDB; private equivalents for condos)

## Legal

- [ ] Conveyancing lawyer engaged (typically $2.5k–$5k for residential)
- [ ] OTP terms read and understood (option fee 1%, exercise typically 14 days for private, 21 days for HDB resale)
- [ ] Completion date and timeline mapped
- [ ] Stakeholder funds: who holds the option money and exercise fee
- [ ] Existing mortgage discharge (seller side)
